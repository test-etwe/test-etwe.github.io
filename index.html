The Project

The idea of this project is engaging our students in the process of education and preparing them for their real experiences in the field of their future jobs. Through this project we would like to exchange experiences with our partner schools in the field of entrepreneurship by employing our knowledge and multiple creative skills. We have to be aware of the labour market demands and give our students the opportunity to get necessary entrepreneurship skills, knowledge, and experience through preserving each country's cultural tradition. Having all this in mind, students have set up their own training firms which they run as real entrepreneurs. The emphasis is also on the development of social entrepreneurship and raising awareness of social issues through setting up enterprises. It is important for the students who have a training firm course to gain sufficient knowledge and practical skills in order to show their full potentials.
